About Me

The person behind
the work.

I'm a senior at Tulane University's A. B. Freeman School of Business, studying Marketing with minors in Finance and Design. My work lives at the intersection of creative strategy and analytical thinking — I'm just as comfortable building a data visualization dashboard as designing a brand identity from scratch.

I've worked across genuinely different environments: a global financial exchange, a regional credit union, a healthcare tech company, and a university fashion publication. Each has sharpened how I think about brands and the people who connect with them.

A semester studying fashion entrepreneurship and sustainability at Copenhagen Business School reinforced what I already believed: the best marketing is both culturally aware and strategically grounded.
